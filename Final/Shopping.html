Shopping!

Grundy is beautiful but shopping here is limited so google maps is going to be your best friend.

We do not have a Walmart near the retreats you will be traveling around 45 minutes if you want to go to Walmart. We do have a Piggly Wiggly, Dollar General, CVS, and many gas stations where you should be able to find everything you need.

Piggly Wiggly

The Piggly Wiggly or as we like to call it "The Pig" is located in Monteagle at
754 W Main St, Monteagle, TN 37356
They supply lots of produce and even have lots of vegan options.

Dollar General

There are TWO Dollar Generals near the retreat. If you are at the retreat at Deer Lick Falls the one in monteagle at 915 W Main St, Monteagle, TN 37356 will be the nearest to you.
If you are at the Retreat at Waters Edge the one in Tracy City at 223 Colyar St, Tracy City, TN 37387 will be closest.

CVS Pharmacy

CVS is very well stocked with everything you might need health or beauty wise. It is located in Monteagle at 340 W Main St, Monteagle, TN 37356
